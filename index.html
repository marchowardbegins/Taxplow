FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What exactly is Taxplow? Software or a coaching program?

Taxplow is a cloud-based platform powered by Outcome-Driven Pricing™. It guides discovery, proposes fees, estimates value, and drives an 80% close workflow—with direct support from our founder.

Will this work if I already have a pricing system?

Yes. Whether you use cost-plus, menu, or value pricing, Taxplow augments it with AI-driven pricing and a sales workflow.

Does this work for bookkeepers / outsourced CFOs?

Yes—Bookkeeping, Tax Prep, Advisory, Outsourced CFO, FP&A, Client Advisory, Payroll, Sales Tax, Bill Pay, Audits, Crypto Taxes, and more.

Will Taxplow replace my CRM or practice management?

No—Taxplow is sales enablement. It hands off smoothly to tools like Karbon/Canopy after the close.
