    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    What exactly is Taxplow? Is this software or a coaching program?

    Taxplow is a cloud-based platform that guides you on the right questions to ask, what you should charge based on our AI-powered Outcome-Driven Pricing™ technology, how much you can potentially save each client vs what they are doing without you, and an engagement system that closes on average 80%. This is not software where you have to figure out yourself or another program where you watch a bunch of help videos or suffer “group coaching”. You get direct support from our founder Marc Howard, which is why it's limited to only 5 firms per month as we scale-up our team.

    Will this work if I already have a pricing system in place?

    If you’ve gotten this far chances are you are starting to question your own pricing system. Whether that’s cost-plus, menu pricing or some variety of value pricing, Taxplow does the heavy lifting and is the only system to combine our AI-powered Outcome-Driven Pricing™ technology and a sales closing workflow.

    I don’t do taxes! Does this work for bookkeepers/outsourced CFOs too?

    Yes! Taxplow uses AI and machine learning and data from millions of accounting transactions and engagements to cover just about every firm service, so that you are confident that you are not leaving money or opportunities on the table when it comes to closing highly profitable business clients. This includes client discovery questions and dynamic pricing for:

    • Bookkeeping
    • Tax Preparation
    • Tax Advisory
    • Outsourced CFO
    • FP&A
    • Client Advisory
    • Payroll
    • Sales Tax
    • Bill Pay
    • IRS Tax Audits
    • Cryptocurrency Taxes
    • Personal Finance/Wealth Management
    • and many more...

    But I have a practice management/CRM/proposal system already–will Taxplow replace this?

    Yes and no. Taxplow is not a practice management system nor a CRM but a AI-powered Outcome-Driven Pricing™ sales enablement platform and in fact works smoothly with existing platforms like Karbon or Canopy once the sales is closed and work is ready to be scheduled. In fact, some of our members use Taxplow instead of a fancy CRM to track sales opportunities, however you do not need to replace your existing CRM to use Taxplow.

    Is Taxplow the same as having a list of questions that I ask on every call?

    No. Taxplow uses machine learning to match what services your firm already offers to the questions you will ask clients. It lets you ask in a dynamic, conversational style that is purposely designed to build rapport and immediately make a great impression while always showing your value before your price.

    What if I don't have any leads? Does Taxplow get leads for me too?

    No Taxplow is not a marketing or "lead gen" service. Most of our members already have a steady lead source(s) and are looking to improve their closing rate, ensure they are charging the best fee possible and/or reprice existing clients with a reliable recurring monthly fee and escape the hassle of hourly fees. We optionally work with your existing marketing person and/or lead generation agency for the perfect hand-off from your first client meeting all the way to closing. If you already generate leads internally or via referrals, Taxplow makes it easier for high-value clients to say "Yes" to more profitable engagements with your firm. As part of our service, we help you analyze your existing lead flow to ensure you are getting the most qualified leads to maximize your chances of winning the most valuable and highest margin business clients and HNWIs.
